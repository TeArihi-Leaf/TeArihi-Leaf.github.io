Manaakitanga (Hospitality and Care)

The concept of manaakitanga, or hospitality, is integral to Māori culture. It teaches the importance of caring for others, showing kindness, and offering support to those in need...

Aroha (Love and Compassion)

Aroha, meaning love and compassion, is at the core of Māori culture. It reflects the idea that love is a guiding force that transcends any challenges or hardships we may face...
