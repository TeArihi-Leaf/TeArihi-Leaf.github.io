My Journey

As a Māori software developer, my journey has been one of blending traditional knowledge with modern technology. Every step I take in learning and gaining new skills brings me closer to my goal of contributing to the future of Aotearoa through innovation and software development. It's a journey of resilience, growth, and continuously seeking knowledge to make an impact both in the tech world and my Māori community.
