My Self-Development Journey

As I walked through the path of life, I felt a calling to learn the art of software development.

Over time, I honed my skills, but I also recognised the importance of human skills to succeed in the tech world. I’ve been cultivating a balance between:

  • Technical Skills: Coding, Problem Solving, Debugging
  • Human Skills: Empathy, Communication, Teamwork, Leadership

This journey has transformed me into a better version of myself, both as a developer and as a human being.
